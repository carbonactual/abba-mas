## Purpose

HAPI is the human in the Carbon Actual ecosystem.

This repository will hold the full HAPI build because HAPI is not a single page or feature. It is the human protocol, identity shell, relationship layer, day-to-day activity context, proof system, commitment context, group connection layer, personal AI context, and entry point into the ecosystem.

## HAPI product boundary

HAPI is the human.
Actual is the human's day-to-day lived activity.
Becoming is the curation feed that guides the next movement.

The HAPI repository owns the human object, human-facing shell, and all contracts needed to connect HAPI to Actual, Becoming, ABBA, SEAL, Root, Index, Vault, I/O, Scale, Base Value, Group AI, Entity AI, and Continuum.
