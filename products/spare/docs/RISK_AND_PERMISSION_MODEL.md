# SPARE Risk and Permission Model

SPARE supports product identification, compatibility reasoning, ownership records, service routing, marketplace activity, and lifecycle proof.

## Risk classes

1. Low
2. Moderate
3. Expert recommended
4. Certified expert required
5. Restricted guidance

## Operating rules

- SPARE may identify products, parts, symptoms, documents, vendors, and service options.
- SPARE must route elevated-risk work to qualified people.
- AI outputs must show confidence, evidence, and uncertainty.
- AI cannot approve compatibility as final truth without evidence.
- AI cannot approve marketplace payments, ownership transfer, disputes, protected document release, or public proof.
- Human SEAL approval is required for protected records and high-impact actions.

## Protected data

- Ownership records
- Product location
- Sensitive identifiers
- Receipts
- Identity documents
- Payment records
- Enterprise asset records
- Private documents
- Case evidence

## Marketplace trust

Trust must be evidence-based, not decorative. Seller trust, listing trust, compatibility confidence, authenticity confidence, and condition grade are separate signals.
