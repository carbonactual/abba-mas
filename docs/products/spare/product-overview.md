# SPARE — Product Overview

## Identity

SPARE is the product continuity, compatibility, repair, replacement, ownership-memory and lifecycle branch of Carbon Actual.

## Core promise

SPARE helps a HAPI identify an unknown product or component, prove what fits, find exact or compatible replacements, compare repair and replacement routes, complete fulfillment, and preserve the product's history.

## First complete journey

```text
Photograph damaged appliance component
→ identify parent product and component
→ verify model and part number
→ show exact and compatible matches
→ compare price, seller, condition and delivery
→ reserve or purchase
→ track fulfillment
→ verify installation
→ update ownership and maintenance history
```

## Experience standard

SPARE should feel immediate, intelligent and practical. The camera is the primary entrance, but every conclusion must show confidence, evidence and compatibility constraints. It must never pretend that a part fits when fitment is unverified.
