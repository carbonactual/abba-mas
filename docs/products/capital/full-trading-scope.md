# CAPITAL — Full Trading Scope and Foundation Integration

## Product position

CAPITAL is the dedicated trading, exchange, market-intelligence, market-access, pricing, negotiation, contracting, clearing, custody, collateral, delivery and settlement product of the Carbon Actual ecosystem.

Trading is implemented in four coordinated modes:

1. Ecosystem-wide trading foundation shared by all products and entities.
2. Embedded trading inside ecosystem products and HAPI journeys.
3. CAPITAL as the specialist multi-market trading product.
4. Trading-as-a-Service for external companies, associations, institutions, governments and platforms.

CAPITAL inherits the Carbon Actual General Foundation. It does not create a separate identity, consent, proof, value or agent universe.

## Foundation inheritance

- `#` identifies HAPIs, AIs, entities, markets, venues, instruments, assets, liabilities, orders, contracts and positions.
- `Root` preserves issuance, origin, ownership, legal basis, product specification and underlying truth.
- `VECTOR` defines investment, liquidity, hedging, procurement, disposal, growth, risk and market objectives.
- `CAPABILITY` determines what an actor may view, quote, bid, offer, execute, approve, clear, custody or settle.
- `COMMITMENT` records bids, offers, mandates, contracts, margin duties, delivery duties and settlement obligations.
- `RELATIONSHIP` links buyers, sellers, brokers, agents, exchanges, issuers, custodians, validators, insurers and regulators.
- `ALLOCATION` assigns capital, collateral, inventory, limits, liquidity, compute, data and execution access.
- `Index` provides instruments, market data, prices, curves, order books, liquidity, benchmarks and searchable intelligence.
- `ATLAS` maps markets, venues, supply chains, warehouses, farms, mines, energy systems, routes and counterparties.
- `Vault` protects keys, mandates, contracts, certificates, invoices, warehouse receipts, KYC records and settlement documents.
- `Pulse` records quotes, orders, bids, offers, matches, fills, deliveries, margin events and market changes.
- `Proof` verifies identity, ownership, quality, quantity, custody, delivery, performance and settlement.
- `Actual` records the current verified holding, liability, position, exposure, inventory or completed trade state.
- `SEAL` governs consent, mandates, limits, compliance, visibility, jurisdiction and approvals.
- `I/O` moves money, assets, collateral, fees, taxes, obligations and settlement value.
- `ABBA` resolves identities, curates markets, forms swarms, routes opportunities, coordinates execution and monitors risk.
- `Omni` investigates public markets, counterparties, regulations, price signals, supply chains and future opportunities.

## Full tradable universe

### Financial markets
- Foreign exchange: spot, forwards, swaps, non-deliverable forwards and options.
- Public equities, private equities, funds, ETFs and indices.
- Government, corporate, municipal and private debt.
- Money-market instruments, deposits, commercial paper and treasury products.
- Derivatives: futures, options, swaps, contracts for difference where lawful, structured products and volatility instruments.
- Receivables, invoices, royalties, revenue shares and other contractual cash-flow rights.

### Physical commodities
- Agriculture, grains, food, livestock, fisheries and forestry.
- Metals, minerals, precious metals, industrial metals and critical minerals.
- Oil, gas, refined fuels, electricity, renewable energy and storage capacity.
- Water rights and other legally tradable resource entitlements.
- Chemicals, fertilizers, industrial inputs and construction materials.

### Circular and recovery markets
- Recyclables, reusable goods, salvage, scrap and recovered components.
- Waste streams, industrial by-products and production residues.
- Refurbished, remanufactured, repaired and repurposed products.
- Secondary raw materials and verified material recovery rights.

### Environmental markets
- Carbon credits, offsets, removals, renewable-energy certificates and biodiversity credits.
- Emissions allowances, avoided-emission claims and climate-performance contracts.
- Water, soil, forestry, ecosystem-service and nature-restoration instruments where valid and lawful.

### Digital and programmable markets
- Cryptocurrencies, stablecoins, tokenized assets and decentralized-finance positions where lawful.
- Data rights, software licences, APIs, compute, storage, bandwidth and digital capacity.
- AI models, agents, capabilities, prompts, inference capacity, training capacity and verified AI services.
- Digital identity rights, credentials and access entitlements subject to SEAL and law.

### Enterprise and operational markets
- Products, spare parts, equipment, machinery, vehicles and fleets.
- Property rights, leases, tenancy interests and facility capacity.
- Logistics, warehousing, route capacity, seat capacity and delivery slots.
- Labour, professional services, managed services and verified human capability.
- Procurement demand, supplier capacity, production capacity and future output.
- Insurance, warranties, guarantees, risk transfer and service-level obligations.

### Intangible and future-value markets
- Intellectual property, patents, trademarks, designs, licences and royalties.
- Media rights, content rights, sponsorship, naming rights and audience access.
- Research, discoveries, ideas, inventions and verified contribution rights.
- Future production, future capacity, future delivery and contingent rights.
- Liabilities, obligations, distressed assets and restructuring opportunities where lawful.

### Restricted and prohibited scope

CAPITAL must enforce jurisdiction-specific restrictions. Human organs, body parts, exploitative human claims, illegal goods, stolen goods, sanctions evasion, prohibited securities activity, unlicensed financial intermediation and unlawful markets are not tradable. Lawful health-services scheduling, blood donation coordination, transplantation logistics and medical-capacity allocation must remain regulated service workflows rather than open trading markets.

## Core state machine

```text
DISCOVERED
→ VERIFIED
→ ELIGIBLE
→ QUOTED
→ ORDERED
→ MATCHED
→ COMMITTED
→ COLLATERALIZED
→ EXECUTED
→ DELIVERED
```

## Safety and governance

- No autonomous use of HAPI or entity capital without explicit scoped mandate.
- Every agent action must have an actor #, scope, limit, purpose and audit trail.
- Trading access is capability-gated by identity, jurisdiction, certification, risk, capital and role.
- Market recommendations must separate evidence, estimates and uncertainty.
- Conflicts of interest, front-running, manipulation, wash trading and insider misuse must be monitored.
- High-risk, leveraged and derivative activity requires additional SEAL levels and human approval.
- Every external integration begins in read-only or sandbox mode before execution is enabled.
