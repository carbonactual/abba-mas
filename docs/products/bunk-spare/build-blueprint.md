# BUNK and SPARE Build Blueprint

## Shared product system

BUNK and SPARE are built as sibling product branches on the same Carbon Actual foundation.

- BUNK protects continuity of place, shelter, land, property and habitat.
- SPARE protects continuity of products, machines, equipment, components and owned objects.

They share identity, search, market, messaging, proof, portfolio, management, maintenance, value, logistics, reputation, support and agent governance.

## Common user journey

```text
Discover
→ identify
→ verify
→ compare
→ save or request
→ contact or offer
→ commit
→ fulfill
→ own or occupy
→ manage
→ maintain
→ repair or improve
→ transfer, replace or exit
→ Proof
→ Actual
```

## BUNK MVP

### Primary routes

1. Discover property by text, map, image, location and saved search.
2. Create a verified listing for sale, rent, shortlet, development or investment.
3. Verify realtor, developer, owner, mandate, location, documents and inspection state.
4. Compare true acquisition cost, affordability, financing and recurring obligations.
5. Make an enquiry, offer, bid or reservation.
6. Track documents, inspections, commitments and handover.
7. Place owned, rented and invested property in the portfolio.
8. Manage rent, maintenance, service charges, tenants, facilities and lifecycle events.

### BUNK modules

- discovery and map
- property identity
- listings and saved demand
- realtor and developer profiles
- title and document evidence
- affordability and finance readiness
- offers, bids and negotiation
- occupancy and tenancy
- portfolio and valuation
- property management
- maintenance and SPARE integration
- investment and land banking
- disputes and remedy
- future habitat states

## SPARE MVP

### Primary routes

1. Identify an unknown product or component using camera, image, text, model number, barcode or serial number.
2. Resolve the parent product, exact component and compatibility requirements.
3. Show exact replacement, compatible alternative, used option, repair route and upgrade route.
4. Verify fitment, authenticity, condition, warranty and seller reputation.
5. Compare price, lead time, location and delivery.
6. Request a part when no listing exists.
7. Purchase, reserve, negotiate or source through an approved professional.
8. Add the product and component to ownership memory.
9. Track repair, maintenance, warranty, replacement and end-of-life events.

### SPARE modules

- camera-first identification
- product and component identity
- compatibility graph
- exact replacement and alternatives
- parts marketplace
- repair network
- ownership memory
- manuals and documentation
- warranty and service records
- maintenance schedules
- logistics and fulfillment
- authenticity and condition proof
- enterprise procurement
- circular reuse and salvage
- lost-item and obsolete-product recovery

## Shared portfolio

The portfolio contains property, products and components under the same subject identity while keeping domain-specific views.

### BUNK portfolio

- owned, rented and managed property
- investments and land-bank interests
- mortgages and obligations
- valuations, income, maintenance and documents

### SPARE portfolio

- owned products and machines
- components and installed parts
- warranties, manuals and service history
- maintenance schedules and replacement forecasts

## Shared agent behavior

ABBA and scoped product agents may search, compare, explain, recommend, route, monitor and prepare actions. They may not invent proof, confirm physical compatibility without evidence, establish ownership, move real value or accept irreversible terms without human authority.
