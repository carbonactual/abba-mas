# MANAGEMENT — Full Ecosystem Management Scope

## Product position

MANAGEMENT is both:

1. A shared management layer used across the Carbon Actual ecosystem.
2. A standalone product that manages entities, operations, people, assets, products, projects, risk, finance, strategy and performance.
3. A family of industry-specific management products.
4. Management-as-a-Service for external entities.

The general MANAGEMENT layer provides the reusable operating model. BUNK, SPARE, Institute GPT, I/O, CAPITAL, Marketplace, Open Polling, CoreFlow and future products inherit it and add domain-specific workflows.

## Foundation inheritance

MANAGEMENT inherits #, Root, VECTOR, CAPABILITY, COMMITMENT, RELATIONSHIP, ALLOCATION, Index, ATLAS, Vault, Pulse, Proof, Actual, SEAL, I/O, ABBA and Omni.

## Human and AI management

The Entity AI may:

- prepare plans and reports
- detect operational gaps and risks
- monitor commitments and dependencies
- forecast capacity and outcomes
- route work and approvals
- preserve institutional memory
- recommend process and role improvements
- coordinate specialist agents

The Entity AI may not silently replace board authority, human consent, professional sign-off, regulated judgment or physical verification.

Human performance must distinguish:

- assigned role delivery
- quality and reliability
- improvement of the role
- contribution beyond the role
- research and foresight
- ecosystem contribution
- structural barriers and resource limits

Management must not reduce human worth to a score or confuse activity with value.

## Management state model

```text
DISCOVERED
→ DEFINED
→ PLANNED
→ RESOURCED
→ AUTHORIZED
→ ACTIVE
→ MONITORED
→ AT RISK OR ON TRACK
→ DELIVERED
→ VERIFIED
→ ACTUAL
→ REVIEWED
→ OPTIMIZED
```

Exception states include blocked, paused, under-resourced, breached, disputed, failed, cancelled, transferred and superseded.

## Non-negotiable rules

- Every managed subject has a persistent #.
- Authority follows roles, appointments and SEAL, not informal influence.
- Plans must connect to resources, commitments and evidence.
- Activity is not automatically performance.
- Major decisions and state changes must be attributable and auditable.
- Every allocation must have purpose, conditions and expected outcomes.
- Every agent must have scoped tools, data, supervisor and revocation.
- Every industry branch inherits the general management foundation.
- Sensitive external entity data must remain tenant-isolated.
- No AI-only inference may establish important physical, legal or financial Actual states.
