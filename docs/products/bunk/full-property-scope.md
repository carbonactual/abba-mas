# BUNK — Full Property, Place, Shelter and Future Habitat Scope

## Product position

BUNK is the complete property and place infrastructure of Carbon Actual. It covers shelter, land, buildings, occupancy, ownership, development, construction, management, maintenance, finance, investment, trading, proof, transfer and future habitation.

BUNK is not only a real-estate listing product. It is the property lifecycle, property market, property management, property investment and future-habitat product of the ecosystem.

## Foundation inheritance

BUNK inherits #, Root, VECTOR, CAPABILITY, COMMITMENT, RELATIONSHIP, ALLOCATION, Index, ATLAS, Vault, Pulse, Proof, Actual, SEAL, I/O, ABBA and Omni.

## Property lifecycle

```text
DISCOVERED
→ SUBMITTED
→ IDENTITY CHECKED
→ DOCUMENTS RECEIVED
→ LOCATION VERIFIED
→ TITLE REVIEWED
→ INSPECTED
→ VALUED
→ AVAILABLE
→ OFFERED
→ NEGOTIATING
→ COMMITTED
→ ESCROWED
→ TRANSFERRED OR OCCUPIED
→ MANAGED
→ MAINTAINED
→ REVALUED
→ TRADED, REFINANCED OR EXITED
→ PROVEN
→ ACTUAL
```

Exception states include incomplete, suspicious, disputed, encumbered, foreclosed, suspended, expired, abandoned, demolished, destroyed and superseded.

## Non-negotiable rules

- Every property must have a persistent #.
- Listing identity is not ownership Proof.
- Digital tokens do not replace legal title.
- Future habitat concepts must not be presented as existing physical property.
- Total acquisition and lifecycle costs must be visible.
- Exact location may be protected by SEAL until authorized.
- Every realtor, developer, manager and professional must have scoped identity, mandate and evidence.
- Off-platform communication must not erase BUNK’s audit and attribution trail.
- High-risk investment and mortgage actions require suitability, jurisdiction and human approval.
- No property becomes Actual from AI inference alone.
