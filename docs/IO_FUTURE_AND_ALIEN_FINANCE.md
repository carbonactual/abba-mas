# I/O Future and Alien Finance

I/O includes research and simulation domains for future financial systems and speculative non-human or interplanetary exchange systems.

## Truth boundary

Future finance may describe plausible emerging systems.
Alien finance is a speculative design domain. It must never be represented as an existing market, proven civilization, available asset class, or real counterparty unless independently verified.

All speculative records use SIMULATED, FICTIONAL, UNVERIFIED, CONDITIONAL, or JURISDICTION_RESTRICTED truth states until stronger evidence exists.

## Future finance scope

- autonomous agent commerce with human-governed limits
- machine-to-machine settlement
- programmable money and tokenized deposits
- central-bank digital currency adapters
- stablecoin and bank-deposit interoperability
- unified-ledger models
- privacy-preserving identity and compliance proofs
- account abstraction and intent-based transactions
- micropayments and streaming payments
- usage-based finance
- compute, storage, bandwidth, energy, and data credits
- AI model, agent, workflow, and capability royalties
- dynamic insurance and parametric protection
- climate, biodiversity, carbon, water, and environmental accounting
- future-income and capability-backed pathways
- cooperative and community capital
- decentralized physical infrastructure finance
- fractional rights where lawful
- programmable escrow and milestone finance
- real-time treasury and liquidity routing
- quantum-resistant migration planning
- post-quantum signatures and key rotation
- offline, delay-tolerant, and intermittent-network settlement
- cross-border and multi-jurisdiction settlement simulations
- planetary, lunar, orbital, and cislunar commerce models
- resource-rights and service-rights accounting
- robotics, drone, satellite, and autonomous-fleet finance
- longevity, health-outcome, education-outcome, and capability finance
- reputation-assisted but never reputation-only underwriting

## Alien finance scope

Alien finance is a governed thought-experiment and interoperability research layer for possible exchange with unknown non-human intelligences, machine civilizations, synthetic societies, or off-world communities.

Research objects may include:

- non-fiat value systems
- energy-denominated exchange
- matter, information, time, entropy, computation, attention, coordination, access, and survival value
- radically different scarcity models
- non-human ownership and stewardship models
- collective or hive agency
- delayed communication and long-settlement horizons
- relativistic timing and clock disagreement
- unknown units, symbols, languages, and number systems
- incompatible concepts of property, debt, consent, identity, and personhood
- proof systems without shared institutions
- exchange under no common law
- quarantine, contamination, and biosecurity constraints
- asymmetric information and unknowable intent
- first-contact non-exploitation rules
- reciprocal observation before exchange
- no automatic recognition of claims, assets, authority, or currency
- reversible sandbox exchange before irreversible transfer
- treaty, scientific, ethical, and planetary-governance review

## Mandatory safeguards

- no claim that extraterrestrial counterparties exist
- no public sale of fictional alien assets as real investments
- no guaranteed valuation
- no irreversible transfer during research simulations
- no autonomous first-contact negotiation
- no weapon, biological, surveillance, or coercive exchange route
- no human, community, planetary, or ecological rights may be traded away
- no recognition of ownership over planets, species, persons, cultures, or sovereign territories without lawful authority
- every scenario requires truth-state, evidence, assumptions, uncertainty, reversibility, and human approval

## Architecture

Future and alien finance run through the same I/O boundaries:

Source → Classification → Truth State → Evidence → Scenario → Simulation → Risk Review → Human Approval → Bounded Experiment → PULSE → PROOF → Actual.

No speculative scenario may bypass SEAL, SHIELD, Vault, PROOF, or human final authority.
