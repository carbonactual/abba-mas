# Carbon Actual Universal Ecosystem Architecture

This directory is the canonical classification and integration map for Carbon Actual industries, products, businesses, entities, HAPIs, AIs, management services, and future domains.

## Operating doctrine

1. Every industry is represented as a domain.
2. Every domain contains specialised, independently deployable products.
3. Products are built around the real entities, relationships, workflows, risks, obligations, markets, and proof conditions of their industries.
4. Every entity can integrate with Actual Management through a common management envelope.
5. Every HAPI retains one continuous identity while participating across multiple domains.
6. Shared ecosystem services are inherited rather than rebuilt by every product.
7. SEAL limits access and action by identity, authority, consent, purpose, and evidence.
8. Root preserves foundational truth; VECTOR records direction; Pulse records movement; Proof verifies; Actual records achieved reality.
9. The past is used for proof, continuity, recovery, and learning; the present for action; the future for survival, resilience, opportunity, and advancement.
10. Every product and management service must include future forecasting, foresight, scenario planning, and frontier readiness.
