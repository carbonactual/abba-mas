# Actual Management — Management as a Service

Actual Management is the universal management, coordination, operations, intelligence, and evidence layer of Carbon Actual. It works inside the ecosystem and can be sold externally as software, co-managed operations, fully managed services, fractional management, embedded management offices, and outcome-managed services.

## Canonical parent-and-branch doctrine

Actual Management is the complete parent management system of the ecosystem. It contains the universal management capabilities required to govern, operate, coordinate, protect, finance, measure, improve, recover, and prepare any managed subject.

Every specialised management system is a branch of Actual Management. A branch is not a disconnected management product and does not rebuild the common foundation. It inherits the universal management core, then adds the entities, terminology, workflows, standards, risks, controls, interfaces, agents, reports, forecasts, and Proof conditions required by its industry or subject.

## Canonical rule
Actual Management owns the complete scope of management.
