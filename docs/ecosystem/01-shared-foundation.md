## Reverse-economic doctrine
The ecosystem does not merely extract value from entities. It identifies liabilities, missing capabilities, blocked potential, relationships, knowledge, assets, and opportunities, then curates support toward verified Becoming. Optimisation is progressive and tied to responsible effort, movement, milestones, proof, and traction.
