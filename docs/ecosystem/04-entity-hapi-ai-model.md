# Universal Entity, HAPI and AI Model

## Entity classes
- Human: HAPI, worker, customer, citizen, patient, learner, tenant, owner, investor, professional, creator, volunteer, beneficiary, guardian, dependant.
- AI: personal AI, organisational AI, product AI, specialist agent, tutor, manager, auditor, marketplace agent, swarm, automated process, synthetic representative.
- Organisation: company, institution, department, team, branch, subsidiary, partnership, association, cooperative, agency, consortium, franchise, project office, community.
- Physical: land, property, room, building, vehicle, machine, equipment, product, component, inventory, warehouse, infrastructure, farm, vessel, aircraft, spacecraft, biological and natural resources.
- Digital: software, application, platform, dataset, model, API, repository, domain, account, content, certificate, wallet, token, digital asset and identity.
- Financial: account, transaction, loan, collateral, investment, fund, currency, security, commodity, invoice, budget, credit, liability, royalty and policy.
- Legal: contract, licence, permit, title, right, claim, obligation, judgement, policy, regulation, trust, estate and intellectual property.
- Operational: task, workflow, project, programme, portfolio, case, incident, order, appointment, service request, work order, inspection, approval and shipment.
- Environmental: ecosystem, species, habitat, emission, waste stream, water source, forest, reserve, carbon unit and restoration obligation.
- Future: forecast, scenario, future asset, planned habitat, autonomous fleet, synthetic organism, planetary resource, future contract and long-term obligation.

## Common management envelope
Every entity may carry: `#`, type, domain memberships, Root, VECTOR, past/present/future state, ownership, authorities, relationships, permissions, assets, liabilities, obligations, processes, risks, opportunities, Pulse, Proof, Actual, value state, reputation, next action and next unlock.

## HAPI doctrine
A HAPI is not merely an account. A HAPI is a continuous human entity with rights, dignity, authority, memory, value, direction, relationships and participation across domains.

A HAPI may simultaneously participate as a learner, worker, owner, tenant, patient, customer, citizen, investor, creator, seller, borrower, policyholder, member, founder or caregiver without creating duplicate core identities. Domain views are permission-scoped through SEAL.

## HAPI operating environment
- identity, Root, VECTOR and trusted representation
- personal management office, calendar, tasks, obligations and documents
- finance, credit, insurance, investments, tax, pension and business interests
- work, skills, learning, credentials, portfolio, career and opportunities
- companies, products, customers, suppliers, staff, projects and succession
- property, devices, vehicles, warranties, maintenance and SPARE
- health administration, care providers, appointments, dependants and emergency information
- family, relationships, community, civic rights, licences, permits and public services
- creative works, ownership, licences, royalties, audience and reputation
- travel, mobility, bookings, routes, insurance and future relocation
- personal marketplace and trading participation
- past recovery, present action and future scenario planning

## AI entity requirements
Every AI must have an identity, issuer, bound HAPI or organisation where applicable, domain, purpose, scope, capabilities, prohibited actions, data permissions, model and training lineage, costs, tasks, decisions, outputs, failures, Pulse, Proof, Actual, audit history, revocation and retirement process.

No AI or agent receives universal ecosystem access. ABBA coordinates scoped agents; SEAL and authorised humans retain final authority.

## Relationship graph
Relationships are first-class objects: owns, controls, represents, employs, serves, supplies, teaches, treats, insures, finances, occupies, maintains, transports, certifies, regulates, depends-on, creates, owes, pays, verifies and benefits.
