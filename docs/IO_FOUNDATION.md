# I/O Foundation

I/O is the shared financial and value operating layer of Carbon Actual.

## Governing expression

Carbon indexes value. Actual identifies Becoming. I/O activates the financial structure. PULSE records movement. PROOF confirms reality. Actual records what it became.

## Foundation rules

- Human authority is final.
- Production execution is disabled by default.
- Every value record carries an explicit truth state.
- Every provider is optional, scoped, revocable, and policy-gated.
- Private identity and credentials remain outside public records.
- Products reuse shared identity, consent, proof, marketplace, communication, and ledger contracts.
- Direct Bank is the first operational container; I/O remains the larger ecosystem layer.

## Initial modules

Command, Index, Financial Body, Pathways, Nano, PabloPay, Earn-to-Pay, Subscriptions, Providers, Settlement, Ledger, Treasury, Risk, SHIELD, BE, Proof, Blockchain, Media Rights, Resource Accounting, and SDK contracts.

## Collectibles

BE is the first canonical ABBA-governed collectible class. Classes two through nine remain reserved until separately defined. A collectible may hold proof, edition, rarity, weight, rights, and acceptance metadata. It never guarantees a price, transfer, authority, settlement, or acceptance.
