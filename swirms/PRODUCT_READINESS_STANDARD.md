# ABBA MAS Product Readiness Standard

Status: pre-product foundation.

A product may enter build only when the Swirm team, data contract, activation state, risk level, and proof route are known.

## Required before building any product

1. Product name and ecosystem layer.
2. Primary user: human, company, institution, agent, community, government, creator, operator, or developer.
3. Product class: app, gateway, dashboard, agent, protocol, marketplace, workflow, API, course, finance layer, proof layer, or field system.
4. Required Swirms.
5. Minimum providers.
6. Data schema.
7. Command router path.
8. SEAL level.
9. Proof route.
10. Monetization path.
11. Launch surface: website, app, API, WhatsApp, dashboard, field form, browser, or agent gateway.

## Product lifecycle

- idea
- scoped
- swirm_team_formed
- schema_ready
- prototype_ready
- sandbox_live
- pilot_live
- production_live
- strengthened
- scaled

## Product build rule

No product should depend on an integration marked only as `candidate` or `scouted`.

Allowed dependency states for product MVP:

- registry_ready for non-critical optional providers
- credentials_pending for manual/sandbox placeholders
- sandboxed for internal tests
- connected for live internal workflows
- pilot_active for external controlled pilot
- production_active for scale
