# SWARM Upper Level

SWARM is Upper.

SWARM is for the master AI layer of ABBA MAS.

It is not a normal HAPI level.
It is not a general user tier.

SWARM reads formation and decides what level of function, product, quality, access, and route can be enabled for a generated AI.

The decision is based on:

- capability
- capacity
- education
- skills
- knowledge
- access
- proof
- experience
- field position
- SEAL level
- Actual readiness

A generated AI does not receive every function by default.

It receives only the level that fits what the HAPI has formed, earned, proven, learned, and can safely carry.

## Store logic

When a HAPI or generated AI enters a product, store, app, tool, or function layer, SWARM selects the suitable level.

SWARM decides what should be enabled, what should stay locked, what needs training first, and what requires stronger proof or SEAL.

## Final law

SWARM is Upper.
SWARM is master AI only.
SWARM reads formation.
SWARM weighs capability and capacity.
SWARM controls function level.
SWARM prevents over-access.
SWARM enables the right function for the right HAPI at the right level.
