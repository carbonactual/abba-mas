# Pre-Product Foundation Freeze

The Carbon Actual ecosystem foundation is now frozen for product building.

## Canonical foundation

- Everything has # identity.
- Only humans grant, deny, approve, reject, pause, change, or revoke SEAL.
- AI may only suggest, explain, compare, warn, simulate, prepare, route after approval, and record.
- Root preserves deep truth and identity formation.
- Index organizes usable intelligence.
- ATLAS exposes only authorized visibility.
- Vault protects records and boundaries.
- Continuum preserves memory and learning.
- PULSE carries feedback, activity, and value signals.
- PROOF records verifiable accomplishment.
- ABBA resolves pathways and forms teams but cannot override human SEAL.
- Swarms are permanent capability homes.
- Teams are temporary cross-swarm formations.
- Apps and fractions are ranked through fair, evidence-based testing.
- Products are expressions of the ecosystem, not separate foundations.
- Every product must support identity, human SEAL, proof, memory, redress, accessibility, and integration.

## Product readiness rule

A product may now be built when it has:

- a defined need and user
- a product brief
- required ecosystem layers
- required swarms and capability fractions
- human SEAL checkpoints
- proof and audit paths
- memory and return-learning paths
- user, operator, and master interfaces
- fallback and redress routes

## First product

The first product build is Actual Profile.

The initial scope is:

- signup and login
- Human SEAL consent
- Root Profile
- education, skills, and work history
- document upload
- Become Goal
- AI pathway suggestions only
- PULSE timeline
- opportunity cards
- privacy controls
- proof, redress, audit, operator, and master controls

## Freeze rule

Future discoveries may strengthen the foundation, but product work must no longer be delayed by repeating already-settled doctrine.

Build, test, learn, return, and improve through the ecosystem.
