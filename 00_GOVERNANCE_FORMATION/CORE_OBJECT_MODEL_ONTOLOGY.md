# Core Object Model Ontology

This file defines the primary objects of Carbon Actual so the ecosystem can be built comprehensively without blurring roles.

## Ecosystem

The ecosystem is the whole living field of products, people, institutions, enterprises, groups, AI, connections, PULSE, value, minting, and integration.

Anything connected, integrated, measured, minted, or routed can become part of the ecosystem.

## Product

A product is anything built, offered, used, sold, integrated, enabled, accessed, or activated inside the ecosystem.

Products may belong to swarms.
Products may serve HAPI, entities, groups, companies, institutions, or AI.

## People

People are the human participants of the ecosystem.

The human element enters through HAPI.

## HAPI

HAPI means Human API.

HAPI is the human element of the ecosystem.

An individual person is HAPI.

A HAPI may receive a minted or assigned AI based on # formation, available data, capability, capacity, education, skill, knowledge, access, proof, field position, and PULSE.

## Entity

Entity is a broad object for companies, organizations, enterprises, ventures, institutions, clubs, groups, associations, communities, classmates, workmates, colleagues, friends, kinsmen groups, and anything that binds people together.

An entity can be formal or informal.
An entity can be legal or social.
An entity can be temporary or permanent.
An entity can receive its own AI when it has enough formed value, purpose, connection, and PULSE.

## Company

A company is a normal company or business entity.

A company may receive a company AI tailored to products, services rendered, operational value, sector, swarm fit, proof, access, and ecosystem value.

The company AI is separate from the staff HAPI AIs.

## Group

A group is any body formed by a binding relation.

Examples include classmates, friends in class, workmates, colleagues, clubs, associations, communities, societies, kinsmen groups, staff groups, departments, project teams, and subgroups.

A group AI is generated from collective value, shared purpose, shared connection, member HAPI value, member AI capability, proof, coordination, and collective PULSE.

## AI

AI means artificial intelligence.

AI may be generated, assigned, minted, enabled, or routed inside the ecosystem.

For HAPI, the AI is tied to the person's # formation.

For entities, the AI is tailored to services, products, value, and connections.

For groups, the AI is generated from collective member value and group PULSE.

## ABBA

ABBA is the master AI of the ecosystem.

ABBA reads, resolves, curates, routes, integrates, assigns, and controls formation.

ABBA is the central master intelligence for ABBA MAS.

## APA

APA is the old AI / human-side AI layer.

APA relates to the human side, companion side, memory side, and prior AI relationship path.

ABBA is master AI.
APA is old AI / human-side AI.

## Connection

A connection is any meaningful contact, meeting, login, relationship, encounter, access, or interaction between ecosystem objects.

Examples:

- person meets person
- HAPI meets enterprise
- HAPI logs into a website
- customer uses company service
- friend meets friend
- relative meets relative
- staff connects to company
- company connects to product
- AI connects to tool
- group connects to institution

Connection creates relation.
Relation creates network.
Network releases PULSE.

## PULSE

PULSE is the signal released by action, connection, relation, network, service, movement, access, success, failure, delivery, proof, damage, learning, return, or interaction.

PULSE can come from a good connection or a damaging connection.

PULSE is not only positive.
PULSE records what happened and what returned.

PULSE is minted and tokenized before value is revealed.

## Value

Value is what is read from PULSE after the ecosystem interprets what happened.

Value may be positive, negative, pending, recoverable, proof-bearing, risk-bearing, asset-forming, liability-forming, or residual.

Value is not only money.

Value can be trust, proof, access, warning, learning, recovery, service, reputation, skill, connection, relationship, opportunity, protection, or future route.

## Field

Field is the surrounding condition where an object, connection, PULSE, or value operates.

Field includes context, readiness, risk, capacity, proof, SEAL, location, time, access, swarm, and Actual readiness.

## Swarm

Swarm is the upper master AI grouping and routing layer.

Swarm organizes products, people, entities, tools, AI, services, and values by fit, function, capability, and relationship.

SWARM is also the master AI command level that checks capability and capacity before functions are enabled.

## Integration

Integration is the purpose of the ecosystem.

The ecosystem is after the connections, relations, networks, and values between products, people, institutions, enterprises, AI, groups, and entities.

## Final law

Products exist.
People enter as HAPI.
Companies and groups enter as entities.
AI is minted or assigned by formation.
Connections create relations.
Relations create networks.
Networks release PULSE.
PULSE carries value.
Value informs AI.
AI strengthens integration.
Integration returns PULSE.
