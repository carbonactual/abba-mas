# Commitment and Pledge Full Scope

Commitment is dedicated value toward Becoming.

Pledge is declared commitment.

Commitment is how the ecosystem gets things done.

## Core scope

Commitment includes time, presence, movement, service, money, skill, buying, appearance, logistics, introduction, coordination, network, social participation, family support, event support, vendor patronage, and self-development.

Pledge includes any declared promise to give, attend, serve, pay, buy, wear, bring, sponsor, drive, cook, organize, introduce, support, or deliver.

## Commitment flow

Commitment made.
Value dedicated.
Pledge declared where applicable.
Field records context.
PULSE released.
Presence, service, purchase, or action happens.
Return PULSE comes back.
Value is read.
Proof is attached.
Network grows or changes.
Formation updates.
Becoming moves.

## Presence value

Some value is created only by being there.

Weddings, naming ceremonies, burials, birthdays, graduations, launches, school events, work events, family events, community events, and meetings are commitment fields.

The person's body at the place is part of the value.

Presence gives time, transport, attention, energy, appearance, social proof, and network access.

## Service value

Service commitment includes serving food, driving, cooking, dancing, arranging seats, carrying items, coordinating guests, recording media, making calls, running errands, managing vendors, helping family, hosting guests, escorting elders, and representing a group.

The event moves because someone served.

## Pledge value

A pledge creates expectation and proof route.

A pledge may be fulfilled, partly fulfilled, delayed, converted, transferred, withdrawn, failed, forgiven, or disputed.

Fulfilled pledge increases proof and value.
Delayed pledge creates pending PULSE.
Converted pledge records the new value form.
Failed pledge still releases PULSE as learning, warning, reputation change, opportunity loss, or recovery route.

## Vendor value

Commitment can activate markets.

A person may pledge to wear brocade and buy from a seller.
A person may buy perfume from a seller.
A person may sponsor food, transport, media, decoration, or gifts.

These commitments connect HAPI, vendor, product, event, family, group, and network.

## Opportunity lock-in

Commitment can place a person inside opportunity.

By going, serving, buying, introducing, joining, or fulfilling a pledge, a person may enter the field where a startup, job, marriage, partnership, vendor route, client route, group, or new AI formation emerges.

## Final law

Commitment is value dedicated before full return.
Pledge is commitment declared before fulfillment.
Presence is value.
Service is value.
Buying is value.
Vendor patronage is value.
Appearance is value.
Social distance reduction is value.
Event participation is value.
PULSE records the commitment.
Return PULSE reveals the value.
Becoming moves through commitment and pledge.
