# Connection, Network, Integration, and Minted AI Law

The ecosystem is after integration.

Every connection matters.
Every relation matters.
Every network matters.
Every connection can release PULSE.
Every PULSE carries value.

## Core law

A connection forms a relation.
A relation creates a network.
A network releases PULSE.
PULSE carries value.
Value informs AI minting.
Minted AI strengthens integration.
Integration returns PULSE.

## Everyone can receive AI

Every eligible participant can receive a minted or assigned AI.

This applies to:

- individual HAPI
- enterprise
- company
- institution
- club
- association
- community
- kinsmen group
- staff group
- subgroup
- product group
- service group
- partner entity

The AI is not generic.
The AI is formed by value, connection, relation, capacity, capability, proof, and PULSE.

## Individual law

An individual is HAPI.

HAPI scales through PULSE.
HAPI AI is shaped by the person's formation, access, learning, skills, proof, service, relationship, and return PULSE.

## Entity law

An enterprise, company, or institution receives AI based on services rendered, products offered, operational value, ecosystem value, proof, access, sector, and swarm fit.

## Group law

A group, club, association, community, or kinsmen body receives AI based on collective member value, shared purpose, relationships, proof, service, coordination, and collective PULSE.

## Network law

The ecosystem does not only measure isolated actors.
It measures the relations between them.

Person to person.
Person to company.
Company to company.
Group to group.
Institution to community.
AI to AI.
Product to product.
Service to service.

These relations create the network value the ecosystem is built to discover, integrate, and scale.

## Final law

No connection is empty by default.
No relation is ignored by default.
Every network can release PULSE.
PULSE is value-bearing.
AI is minted or assigned according to formed value.
Integration is the purpose.
