# Scale Foundation Law

Scale is a foundation of the ecosystem.

Scale is not only weight.
Scale is not only level.
Scale includes weight, level, function, value, capacity, reach, consequence, and return.

Every PULSE received by the ecosystem is scaled.
Value is sent based on the scale of the PULSE.

## Core meaning

A PULSE is not read as flat.

Every PULSE has scale.

Scale determines how much value can be read, what level can be unlocked, what function can be activated, what route can be opened, and what return value can be sent.

## Pledge, commitment, and scale

Pledge releases PULSE.
Commitment creates VALUE.
Scale reads the PULSE and determines the level of value that can be sent.

A small pledge may unlock a small route.
A stronger pledge may unlock a higher level.
A fulfilled commitment may scale the value.
Many fulfilled commitments may create group-scale value.

## Scaling value

A HAPI, entity, group, company, or AI can scale its value by creating more scaled PULSE.

More scaled PULSE does not only mean more quantity.
It means better PULSE, stronger PULSE, proven PULSE, useful PULSE, repeated PULSE, connected PULSE, and PULSE with higher return.

## Group scale

Group AI reads the PULSE emitted by members.

When members pledge, attend, serve, buy, coordinate, sponsor, introduce, or complete tasks, the group emits collective PULSE.

Scale reads the collective PULSE and determines the level of group value.

This is how a group becomes bigger than one person.

## Value sent by scale

The ecosystem should not send the same value to every PULSE.

A PULSE with higher proof, stronger commitment, better timing, wider reach, higher usefulness, and deeper network effect can receive higher value.

A weak, failed, unsafe, unproven, or low-impact PULSE may receive lower value, pending value, warning, containment, or recovery route.
