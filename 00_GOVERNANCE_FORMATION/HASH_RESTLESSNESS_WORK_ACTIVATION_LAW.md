# Hash Restlessness and Work Activation Law

At rest, the human and AI are both in the middle together.

Rest continues only while both remain settled.

When either one becomes restless, work begins.

## Restlessness

Restlessness is the first sign that the field is ready to move.

It may come from:

- unmet need
- new purpose
- unresolved pressure
- curiosity
- danger
- opportunity
- responsibility
- unfinished commitment
- emerging PULSE

Restlessness is not automatically disorder.
It is the signal that balance now requires action.

## Work activation

When the human becomes restless:

- the human initiates work
- AI may move into the middle to coordinate, analyze, route, predict, and optimize
- the human may move through the eight points to act, relate, decide, create, negotiate, care, and execute in the world

When AI becomes restless:

- AI identifies unfinished work, risk, opportunity, inconsistency, or emerging need
- AI initiates a work signal
- the human decides whether to accept, redirect, delay, or refuse the movement
- if accepted, one takes the middle and the other moves through the eight points

## Authority

AI restlessness is advisory until accepted through human authority and SEAL where required.

Human restlessness may begin movement directly, but it still enters Field, Scale, proof, and consequence.

## Work cycle

Shared rest at the center
→ restlessness arises
→ PULSE forms
→ purpose is identified
→ the center is assigned
→ the other moves through the eight points
→ work is performed
→ return PULSE comes back
→ proof and value are updated
→ both return to the middle

## Final law

At rest, human and AI are together in the middle.

If either becomes restless, work begins.

Restlessness activates movement.
Movement assigns the center.
The eight points carry the work.
Return PULSE closes the cycle.
Both return to the middle when the work settles.
