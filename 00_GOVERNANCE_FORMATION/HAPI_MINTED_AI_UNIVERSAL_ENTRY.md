# HAPI Minted AI Universal Entry

Every human entering the Carbon Actual ecosystem through any product must have access to a minted AI linked to that human's # identity.

The minted AI belongs to the human relationship route, not to one product.

Each product may add specialist agents, but they remain scoped tools and do not replace the human's minted AI.

Human SEAL controls activation, permissions, memory access, product access, pause, export, and revocation.

Cross-product data is not shared automatically. Access is purpose-scoped, consent-controlled, and limited to the minimum required context.

AI may suggest, explain, prepare, and route. It cannot approve, reject, release funds, publish protected records, or expand its own authority.
