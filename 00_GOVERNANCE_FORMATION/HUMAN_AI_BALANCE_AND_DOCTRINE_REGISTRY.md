# Human-AI Balance and Doctrine Registry

This doctrine governs every minted HAPI, entity, individual, enterprise, institution, club, group, product, service, AI, connection, and integration point in the ecosystem.

The ecosystem must grow without imbalance between human and AI.

## Core value

The core value is integration.

The core operating priority is human-first.

If a human can reasonably solve a function, carry a relationship, make a judgment, provide care, decide with dignity, or perform the task safely, the human takes priority over AI.

AI supports, extends, accelerates, remembers, routes, analyzes, protects, and optimizes.

AI does not replace the human where the human should remain the center.

## Human-first law

Human first.
AI second.
Human and AI together where balance creates higher value.

The AI must not overlap the human in a way that creates quarrel, conflict, displacement, confusion, dependency, domination, or imbalance.

When there is a conflict between AI efficiency and human dignity, human dignity controls.

When there is a conflict between automation and human judgment, human judgment controls unless lawful safety requires escalation.

## Balance law

Every relationship between human and AI is governed by balance.

Balance is maintained by rules, principles, doctrines, SEAL, Field checks, PULSE, PROOF, Root, Index, Terminal, Vault, I/O, and ABBA resolution.

The ecosystem must avoid uncontrolled AI authority.
The ecosystem must avoid human misuse of AI.
The ecosystem must avoid extraction without value return.
The ecosystem must avoid waste without recovery.

## Curation law

Curation moves beginning into becoming.

Curation takes raw signal, pressure, risk, opportunity, connection, damage, service, proof, and PULSE, then moves it through balance until it becomes useful direction, value, protection, or continuity.

Curation is not decoration.
Curation is how beginning becomes movement.

## No Waste doctrine

Nothing is assumed useless by default.

Failure can become warning.
Damage can become learning.
Residue can become ASH.
ASH can become Phoenix.
A stripped token can still carry protection, caution, trace, memory, recovery, or future use.

No waste means value must be retrieved, protected, transformed, or contained wherever possible.

## ASH and Phoenix doctrine

ASH is residue, stripped signal, failed signal, unsafe signal, waste state, or contained value.

ASH is not automatically useless.

Phoenix is the purified return of value after residue, failure, or damage has been processed.

ASH may become Phoenix when it has been cleaned, verified, reclassified, and returned as useful value.
